Pig Gorl (or someone who sounds similar to her) has been cooing incessantly since I got to Dublin. I have been here for approximately three hours. Pig Gorl is not as annoying on this side of the hills as she is in Castro Valley. Perhaps that is because there are some manlier and more intelligent voice actors talking at me on this side. In Castro Valley, Pig Gorl is louder and there are not as many other voices talking concurrently with her. Dublin has more polluted air and is noisier overall because it is near a busy freeway and there are a lot more people. Civilians are more “low class” and noisy on this side because it’s more urban and less residential. I noticed that civilians like to bother me while I am charging electronics while trying to relax simultaneously in Dublin. The cops are worse in Castro Valley, but the civilians are worse in Dublin. Right now, one of them is idling her SUV in front of me and another is playing very loud obnoxious music. I think “the people” are trying to prevent me from using my laptop in peace. As soon as I open it and start using it, they seem to deliberately disrupt my concentration and tranquility. This is not the first time a Latino girl in a big SUV pulled that stunt. I had to literally yell at her to get her to leave. She had a look on her face like she knew that was the drill; that it is her job to force me to act like a hot head when they initiate fights with me because they hate how chill I am otherwise. Her face was caked in makeup like she’s made of ice cream and has to be kept in a gasoline powered fridge on wheels. When I just want to move on with my life, the low life women have a way of trying to prevent me from doing so. I have criticized Mexican women a lot in the past. I can tell they do not like me and want to give me a hard time every chance they get because that is how they live. They like drama and fighting. Maybe that’s my cue to go back to Castro Valley where the people are indeed more civilized. The people on this side are loud, obnoxious, thuggish attention whores. I feel like I am surrounded by immature little kids disguised as adults. I crave the company and solace of smart, kind, and more mature humans. Maybe I should just go back to Castro Valley (and not the campgrounds, but instead, the golf course entrance or else the church).

What was stressing me out about twenty minutes ago was one of the manly voices suggesting that Alan wants me to devolve into a screaming, crying, tantrum thrower because he is sick of me being so cold, white, and manly. I was told that he desperately needs to make me feel like a bitch in chains being tortured and deprived of the right to look dignified, to have real world accomplishments, and to be forced to have only shallow and depressing thoughts so that he feels more manly in comparison. I have not contacted him since last month (and I called his phone several times but he did not answer). I do not plan on picking up the phone if he calls nor do I intend to contact him. I thought I saw him waiting for me to arrive at Castro Village last week. As I was walking towards the clock tower, I thought I saw him standing there. Some young guy was also sitting on the bench to the side and I thought I heard him say, “You can say whatever you want.” I looked up and felt my heart rate go up and a sense of fight or flight mode take over because that guy who looked like Alan was giving off stressed out vibes. He started walking away as soon as I approached with his phone in hand and entered one of the houses nearby. I thought that perhaps Alan secretly bought a house in Castro Valley so that he can stalk me and control me with more precision. I also have suspected that Alan is responsible for me hearing these voices and that Alan is a cop disguised as a software engineer.

It has been suggested to me that I am married to Alan against my will even though my legal records say I am single and never married. A cop can make up their own rules and enforce them. Perhaps my forced marriage to a cop is an opportunity and excuse for me to also play cop; to make up and enforce my own rules as well.

Anyway, I am trying not to waste any time and I am trying to carefully update my website without ruining my progress. It’s disturbing to me how much other people seem to oppose me working on my primary website instead of on merely complaining about people and waiting in vain for them to stop harassing me. It seems like it does not matter how long I wait for people to stop harassing me. As soon as I do anything other than mope and play lowly street dweller, I get bullied mercilessly for hours at a time.

The silver lining of this situation is that it is forcing me not to be so stuffy in how I write. Let me tell you the truth: I am annoyed at how my writing sounds on my main site. There are lots of run on sentences and tangential comments which makes the whole blog seem disorganized, sloppy, and revealing of a low attention span and bad mood in the author. I want to revise the entire site and make the words more succint, clear, and on topic. Another part of me is screaming in terror that I am going to ruin something that I worked very hard to get as articulate as it is. Now the real difficulties of my job are apparent. It is harder to pretend I do not mind leaving pages in a half baked state than it is to just fix them until I no longer see those pages as flawed in some way.

To be perfectionistic is a luxury because it is very time consuming. I cannot hide that I am this much of a perfectionist. I will just have to accept that my job is fraught with difficulties every step of the way. It’s not an easy A. I do not just get a passing grade for merely trying.
