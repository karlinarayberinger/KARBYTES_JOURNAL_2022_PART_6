On and on the snarky, sniveling, bitching voices go. They have been harassing me nonstop today. Whenever I turn my attention to anything other than this topic, I hear horns honk and people in the background caterwauling and saying messages which sound like the above quotations. I think people are obsessed with preventing me from doing anything other than remaining embittered, deprived of space to hear myself think, cut off from being able to enjoy my hobbies, and forced to look like all I do for a living is walk around trying to get away from the verbal harassment while others call me a shallow, immature beauty whore who is not allowed to enjoy its life until it falls into a crippling depression and goes silent, stops showing off its talents, and settles for some meek and humble customer service role.

As I write this note, I am in the process of backing up a two minute video I made today which I uploaded to Instagram of me playing drums even better than the last session. As usual, when the video was about 60% uploaded using my house Wi-Fi, the upload froze. Hence, in order to complete the upload, I had to walk up the street to use my mobile phone data. People have been pestering nonstop today. Since I woke up this morning I have been drowning in nonstop chattering from people nearby. I noticed that the people around me are more overtly hostile towards my being outside and “showing off” than ever before.

What I think is going on is that I live in a “family first” kind of neighborhood in which adults are not allowed to take residence here unless they pay rent and/or have kids. I can tell that people are most angry at me because I appear to have no job and I spend all of my time doing what I want to do. The people around me want to prevent me from being able to practice my hobbies and improve my skills because they are jealous that I have so much free time and things to do other than be a community servant like they are. (I would go on to suggest that the vast majority of jobs can and should be outsourced to robots, but the people are quick to try to shut down such discourse because the people are addicted to making everyone have to suffer to earn their keep and to have insufficient free time to do anything other than conform to the narrow role the societal matrix forces them to play. I think the people are afraid that I am right about a lot of things. The people seem to be afraid that, if most jobs were outsourced to robots and everyone was guaranteed a minimal basic income, everyone would be free to pursue their interests. The people seem to prefer that they be forced to have too little free time and money to update their knowledge and skills because the people do not like having to be under the kind of pressure I am to be adamantly self improvement oriented rather than resigned to be a passive consumer who is not expected to put themselves out there like I do).

At this point in my life, I seem to have more enemies than friends. Rather than try to make friends, I am going to just try to keep to myself and do the bare minimum to earn my keep. I do not expect people in general to be receptive to what I have to say nor tolerant of my existence unless I stay indoors away from the public eye. The fact that I walk around in public so much makes people uncomfortable. They think I am doing it to show off or to stay fit when I am doing it to be able to travel to regions where I can most easily concentrate on my Internet based hobbies without fueling the demand for continued fossil fuels dependence. (It should be noted that, whenever I bring up concerns about the environment, no one seems to want me to continue and they try to change the subject back to ad hominem shit slinging and fear-based economics. People seem to think that caring about the environment and being concerned about environmental toxicity and climate change due to human reliance on fossil fuels and unsustainable living practices is just another cover up for being a “beauty whore”. The people seem to want to say that environmentalists only want to prevent littering and little else because the people are only concerned about what immediately affects them. They do not seem to want to pay attention to news about how human activities have been negatively effecting the environment and humanity’s long term survival prospects because people seem to just resign themselves to living in the moment and only caring about a very small group of people: their family and friends and to hell with everyone else (and to hell with their prospective grandchildren)).

As an aside, my headphones are broken and I do not want to spend what little money I have saved up to purchase new ones. I have been playing noise generator apps without headphones through the built-in device speakers so that I can mask some of that verbal abuse coming my way.
