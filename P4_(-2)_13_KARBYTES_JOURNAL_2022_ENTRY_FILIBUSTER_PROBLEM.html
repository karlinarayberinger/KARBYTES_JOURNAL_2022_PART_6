I noticed that, as soon as I start to get into a groove where I feel better than normal, that’s usually when my mother (the sole provider of my money) “forgets” to issue a daily allowance and, when I talk to her, she says that she is going to have to start cutting down my allowance within a month (and that she wants me to apply for any job and take the car to get there).

Today I have been easy going and as goody two shoes as possible about the latest “threat” to my increasingly “off the grid” lifestyle. I said that I would apply for jobs (and if and when I do, I plan to look for part time jobs only in nice places where there are lots of studious, smart, and progressive minded people such as Berkeley so that I can spend most of my days doing whatever I want (and I do not intend to apply for any jobs until after 01_SEPTEMBER_2022 so that I can have sufficient time to update my website and establish a sustainable mind-body-spirit fitness routine)).

Right now I have $180 in the bank. I plan to spend as little as possible while I save as much as possible without working. Ideally, I would never let my savings dip below $300.

Anyway, methinks that there are some angry “Hispanics” who want me to stay angry for the same reasons they seem to be: having very little time off from “assembly line” style jobs and having very little money to spare even while working full time at such jobs. I do not mean to be racist. It’s not just about “Hispanics” or any other race. I mean that, the kind of jobs my mom seems to want me to do are ones which make me feel like I have to dress up and behave like a teenage prostitute or, at the very least, assimilate to the “low brow” cultural norms which my less educated, less environmentally conscientious, less holistic, less physically fit, and less solitary “peers” at my job. (When I told my mom I was planning on looking for jobs in Berkeley, she was rather dismissive because I think she secretly wants me to be a Hayward girlfriend to some rich, belligerent thug who watches me and meddles in my affairs form a good safe distance because he has some special position with respect to society (but that could be me getting carried away with conspiratorial thoughts again (or perhaps I am right about at least some of my “conspiracy theories”))).

Anyway, I do not want the pursuit of money and social acceptance to impede my personal projects during the remaining months of summer. I was thinking about writing some kind of “orange page” on my primary website which talks about global ethical precepts such as (a) minimal interventionism, (b) minimal ecological damage, and (c) maximum personal freedom. That is not as high of a priority to me as is revising the web page of my primary website named PUBLIC_DOMAIN. I have been afraid to get into that one because I am afraid that, as soon as I try, other people who are in physical proximity to me are going to try to distract or otherwise deter me. Hence, I spend a significant amount of my time traveling by foot to relatively secluded locations to use my laptop.

One last note: the police stopped to issue me a warning on the side of the road to not camp in the Ramage trail area because that is not a camping area (and that I can camp at Lake Chabot campgrounds). So far, Lake Chabot campgrounds are the best places I know of to legally camp (but on weekends that area tends to get crowded and noisy). Also, once the sun sets and it gets dark, I am afraid that mountain lions will be more likely to stalk and attack me if I decide to leave my tent for any reason. (I sleep in a tent because I think it is sufficient to deter mountain lions from attacking me and because I do not feel comfortable sleeping indoors or where lots of people, lights, and cars are present).

How am I supposed to hold down a job if I am unwilling to sleep in more densely populated regions and use the petroleum powered van to get to work? Perhaps I will find a way to make money 100% remotely such as a data entry job. Otherwise, I would to limit my availability to nights and weekends only so as to not lose the special worldview I worked so hard to cultivate; a worldview which is not so bombarded with societal conditioning which goes against what I stand for.

I will not take a job unless I think it is compatible with my mind-body-spirit routines and preferences. My time is too precious to squander just to appease those who are envious of my relative freedom and well being. I refuse to be another sellout to mainstream society which normalizes chronic depression, overpopulation, solitude avoidance, and intellectual stuntedness (not to mention apathy towards sustainability and environmental toxicity and climate change concerns (because most people around me seem to implicitly believe that I am not a “productive” member of society unless I am part of the petroleum burning, meat eating, wilderness destroying cult which seems to have mass appeal and which seems to suggest that there is no alternative to joining that cult).
